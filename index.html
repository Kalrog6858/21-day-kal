About the 21-Day Habit Challenge

This challenge is designed to help newcomer youth gradually build language skills and adapt to Canadian society through daily activities:

  • Daily Word Practice: Learn a new word every day.
  • Reading and Comprehension: Improve language through reading articles and discussing them.
  • Conversation Practice: Practice speaking and presenting.
  • Daily Gratitude Writing: Develop positive thinking and language skills simultaneously.
  • Weekly Library Visit: Explore books and deepen language understanding.

Weekly Progress

As part of the challenge, youth will engage in various activities every week, including:

  • Discussing and role-playing social scenarios.
  • Learning polite phrases for daily interactions.
  • Pairing up for community outings to practice politeness phrases and observe social norms.

We will track and celebrate progress, encouraging youth to share their experiences and grow together.
