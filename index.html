About the Challenge

This program supports youth in their journey to adapt to life in Canada through structured activities, including language practice, cultural insights, and personal growth.

  • Daily Word Practice: Learn a new word every day.
  • Reading and Comprehension: Improve language through reading articles and discussing them.
  • Conversation Practice: Practice speaking and presenting.
  • Daily Gratitude Writing: Develop positive thinking and language skills simultaneously.
  • Weekly Library Visit: Explore books and deepen language understanding.
